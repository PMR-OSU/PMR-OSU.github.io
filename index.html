Revolutionizing Assisted Living

Introducing the Patient Monitoring Robot, designed to improve the quality of life for the elderly community in America. Enhance safety, well-being, and peace of mind for your loved ones while saving on the high costs of assisted living.

Learn More

Features

Health Monitoring

Advanced sensors and cameras track the movements and overall well-being of the elderly, ensuring their safety and health.

Abnormal Behavior Detection

Identify any abnormal movements or changes in behavior, sending notifications for further evaluation and assistance.

Medication Reminder

Program the robot to remind the elder to take their medications on schedule, ensuring proper adherence to their treatment plan.

Unauthorized Exit Alert

Alert family members and living assistance staff if the elder leaves the facility without permission, ensuring their safety.
